Selected Projects

MmCows: A Multimodal Dataset for Dairy Cattle Monitoring

We introduce comprehensive multimodal dataset for health monitoring dairy cattle called MmCows. It includes data from dairy cows over 14 days consisting 3D UWB location, IMU acceleration, air pressure, body temperature, ankle acceleration, multi-view RGB images, weather conditions, UWB distances, head direction, lying behavior, and health records. Additionally, it features 20,000 annotated isometric-view images from four cameras, detailing cow IDs and behaviors of 16 cows, along with 3D body location ground truth.
